### About our communications progression frameworks
The communications progression framework is a tool that helps content and comms:
- Make development and career plans
- Track personal and professional development
- Talk about progress in a consistent way
- Set a fair level of compensation.

### How does it work?

The framework covers all the things we‚Äôre looking for from the content and communications team. The framework is split into 5 sections, with subsections for subgroups. The sequence will detail a guide to help you track your professional progress.
